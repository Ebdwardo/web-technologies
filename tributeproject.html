Here's a timeline of LeBron James life:

  • 1984 - Lebron was born December 30
  • 2003 - Lebron was drafted with the number one pick by the Cleveland Cavaliers
  • 2005 - Lebron's first triple double was on January 19th
  • 2005, February 20th - Lebron had his first career all star appearance
  • 2008-2009 season - First mvp award
  • 2009-2010 season - Lebron wins his 2nd mvp award
  • 2010, July 8th - Lebron decided to leave Cleveland and join the Miami Heat
  • 2011-2012 season - Lebron wins his 3rd mvp award
  • 2011-2012 season - Lebron won his first ring with the Miami Heat
  • 2012-2013 season - Lebron wins 4th mvp award
  • 2012-2013 season - Lebron wins second ring with the Miami Heat
  • 2014, March 3rd - Lebron scores a career high of 61 points
  • 2014, July 11th - Lebron tells cleveland that he is coming back home to play with the cavaliers
  • 2016, June 19th - Lebron wins his 3rd ring for his hometown team the Cleveland Cavaliers
